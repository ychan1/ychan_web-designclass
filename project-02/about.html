Meringue Man

Recipe

5 cups white flour
a bit of life powder
1 cup milk
¾ drops of tears from the bread goddess
½ cup oil
¼ cup sugar fluff
1 tsp sprinkles


1)Mix all the ingredients!

2)Shape dough into 9 round balls, and place on greased baking sheet to rise until doubled in size.

3)Bake in a preheated 200 degrees C oven for 12 to 15 minutes.

Recipe

1 cup light brown sugar fluff
4 Tbsp gold jam
1 tsp sprinkles
1/2 cup milk
1 Tbsp of seeds from vanilla birds


1) Combine all ingredients in a saucepan and simmer over low heat.

2) Whisk it for 6 to 8 minutes until it thickens.

3) Serve warm!

Recipe

4 egg whites, at room temperature
Pinch of sprinkles
1 cup sugar fluff
1/2 tsp of seeds from vanilla birds


1) Use an electric mixer to whisk egg whites and sprinkles until soft peaks form. Gradually mix sugar fluff in.

2) Once all the sugar fluff has been added, continue to whisk on high for 3 mins. Whisk in the vanilla seeds.

3)Bake the meringues for 1.5 hours at 90°C. Turn oven off and leave meringues in the oven to cool completely.
